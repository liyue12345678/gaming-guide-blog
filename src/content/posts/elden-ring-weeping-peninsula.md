The Weeping Peninsula is the area directly south of Limgrave, and it's hands-down the best place to go after clearing the starting zone. It's packed with upgrade materials, has 4 churches with Sacred Tears, and gives you enough runes to hit level 30 before Stormveil Castle.

Here's everything you need to know.

## Why Visit Weeping Peninsula First

Before you even think about Stormveil Castle, go south. Here's why:

- **4 Sacred Tears** — more than any other early-game area
- **3 Golden Seeds** — get your flasks to 8+ charges
- **Castle Morne** — excellent practice dungeon with a fun boss
- **Erdtree Avatar** — easy boss, drops powerful Crystal Tears
- **Plenty of Smithing Stones [1] and [2]**

This single zone can push you from level 15 to level 30 with minimal risk.

## How to Get There

From the First Step Site of Grace, head south along the main road. You'll cross a Bridge of Sacrifice — be careful of the ballista and soldiers. Once you cross, you're in the Weeping Peninsula.

Grab the map fragment from the obelisk on the hill south of the bridge.

## Key Locations & Bosses

### 1. Church of Pilgrimage (Sacred Tear)

Directly west from the bridge. Grab the Sacred Tear — this is the easiest one.

### 2. Erdtree Avatar (Minor Erdtree)

Northeast section of the peninsula. This boss is weak to fire — use Fire Grease or Flame Sling. Drops the **Opaline Bubbletear** and **Crimsonburst Crystal Tear** for your Physick flask.

### 3. Castle Morne

The main dungeon. Work your way down through the castle — it's linear, hard to get lost. Key items:
- **Claymore** — on a corpse in a courtyard room
- **Twinblade Talisman** — chest near the back
- **Grafted Blade Greatsword** — boss drop

**Boss: Leonine Misbegotten** — aggressive but low HP. Summon the Jellyfish ashes to tank and hit him with charged heavies. Or parry — his attacks are well-telegraphed.

### 4. Churches with Sacred Tears

| Church | Location |
|--------|----------|
| Church of Pilgrimage | West of bridge entrance |
| Fourth Church of Marika | Southwest coast |
| Callu Baptismal Church | South coast, near beach |
| Church of Dragon Communion | Island off west coast (use the tunnel) |

### 5. Night's Cavalry (Night Boss)

On the road between Agheel Lake South and Castle Morne, at night. Drops **Ash of War: Barricade Shield** — fantastic for shield users.

## Hidden Items Not to Miss

- **Lantern** — buy from the merchant near Castle Morne ramp. Hands-free light source!
- **Green Turtle Talisman** — in Summonwater Village (stamina regen, excellent)
- **Zweihander** — from the isolated merchant on the west beach (only 3,500 runes!)

## Recommended Route

1. Cross Bridge of Sacrifice → grab map fragment
2. Church of Pilgrimage → Sacred Tear
3. Minor Erdtree → kill Erdtree Avatar for Physick tears
4. Hit all 3 remaining churches
5. Castle Morne from top to bottom
6. Night's Cavalry on the way out
7. Return to Limgrave around level 30
