Honkai: Star Rail shares DNA with Genshin Impact but plays very differently. The turn-based combat means team composition and speed tuning matter more than reflexes. Here's how to build your account correctly from day one.

## 1. Don't Spend Stellar Jade on the Standard Banner

Same rule as Genshin — the standard banner (Stellar Warp) has no rate-up. Use only free Star Rail Passes. Save every Stellar Jade for limited character banners. The 300-pull standard selector is a marathon, not a sprint.

## 2. Build One Strong Team First

Star Rail requires two teams for endgame (Memory of Chaos, Pure Fiction), but early on, focus everything on 4 characters:

| Slot | Role | Free Option | Best Pull Option |
|------|------|------------|-----------------|
| DPS | Damage dealer | Dan Heng / Serval | Seele / Jingliu / Acheron |
| Support | Buffer / debuffer | Asta / Pela | Ruan Mei / Bronya |
| Sustain | Healer / shielder | Natasha / March 7th | Fu Xuan / Aventurine |
| Flex | Sub-DPS or 2nd support | Tingyun | Robin / Sparkle |

**Priority**: Get a limited 5-star sustain unit first. They make the game dramatically easier. Fu Xuan or Aventurine are the two best investments in the game.

## 3. Trailblaze Power — What to Spend It On

| Level | Priority |
|-------|----------|
| Level 1-30 | Character XP + Credits (you run out fast) |
| Level 30-40 | Trace materials (guaranteed power spike) |
| Level 40-50 | Ascension materials + Light Cone XP |
| Level 50-60 | Start farming relics, but don't go hard yet |
| Level 60+ | Relics become worth farming seriously |

## 4. The Relic Rule — Wait Until Equilibrium 5+

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>⚠️ Key Tip:</strong> Don't heavily farm relics until you unlock the highest difficulty (Equilibrium 5+). Before then, use the Musketeer and Thief sets from weekly bosses and chests. A correct main stat with bad substats is enough for all story content.
</div>

### Relic Priority for Any DPS
1. **Chest**: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (whichever gets you closer to 1:2 ratio)
2. **Boots**: SPD (almost always — speed is the most important stat)
3. **Sphere**: Elemental DMG% matching your character
4. **Rope**: ATK% or Energy Regen Rate (ERR for supports)

## 5. Speed Tuning — The Secret Skill

Speed determines turn order. The breakpoints that matter:

| SPD | Turns per Cycle | Notes |
|-----|----------------|-------|
| 121 | 2 turns in first cycle | Minimum for DPS |
| 134 | 2 turns in first cycle, 2 in second | Sweet spot |
| 161 | 4 turns in first 2 cycles | High investment |

**The golden rule**: Your supports should be faster than your DPS. Buffs need to go up before your damage dealer attacks.

## 6. Which Free Characters Are Worth Building

| Character | Worth It? | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| **Trailblazer (Physical)** | Yes, early | Your first DPS, falls off later |
| **Trailblazer (Fire)** | Absolutely | Top-tier sustain, meta in Super Break teams |
| **March 7th** | Yes | Best free shielder, synergy with Clara/Yunli |
| **Dan Heng** | Situational | Decent wind DPS, but gets outscaled by limiteds |
| **Serval** | Yes | Shock DoT enabler, great for Pure Fiction |
| **Asta** | Yes | Speed buffer, very valuable |
| **Herta** | Absolutely | Queen of Pure Fiction, kuru kuru destroys wave content |
| **Natasha** | Yes, early | Your first healer until you pull a limited sustain |
| **Tingyun** (4-star pull) | Must build | Best 4-star buffer in the game |

## 7. Simulated Universe — Your Weekly Priority

Simulated Universe resets every Monday. Always do your weekly run — it's your main source of relic XP materials and Herta Bonds (for 5-star Light Cones in Herta's Store).

**Best F2P Light Cones from Herta's Store**:
1. **Aeon** (Destruction) — excellent for any Destruction DPS
2. **Stellar Sea** (Hunt) — solid CRIT stat stick
3. **Texture of Memories** (Preservation) — great defensive option

## 8. Don't Over-Cap Your Energy

Trailblaze Power caps at 240. If you let it sit at cap, you lose regeneration. Log in at least once a day to burn it. Even 5 minutes of auto-battling relic domains is better than wasting Power.

## 9. Team Compositions That Work

### Hypercarry (Easiest to Build)
```
DPS + Buffer + Debuffer + Sustain
Example: Dan Heng + Tingyun + Pela + Natasha
```

### Dual DPS (More Investment, More Damage)
```
DPS + Sub-DPS + Support + Sustain
Example: Ratio + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine
```

### DoT (Damage over Time)
```
DoT 1 + DoT 2 + Support + Sustain
Example: Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo
```

### Super Break (Current Meta)
```
Carry + Trailblazer (Harmony) + Ruan Mei + Sustain
Example: Firefly + Trailblazer + Ruan Mei + Gallagher
```

## 10. The 300-Pull Standard Selector

After 300 pulls on the standard banner, you get to pick one 5-star. **Pick Bronya** unless you have a strong reason otherwise. She's the most universally useful standard character — 100% action advance on skill is unique and irreplaceable.
