Margit the Fell Omen is the first major skill check in Elden Ring. Many new players hit a wall here — his delayed attacks, aggressive combos, and tight arena make him feel overwhelming.

This guide breaks down everything you need to know to beat him.

## Preparation: Before You Enter the Arena

### Recommended Level and Gear

- **Level**: 20-25 (at least 20 Vigor)
- **Weapon Upgrade**: +2 to +3
- **Flask**: 5-6 charges, at +2 strength

### Get Margit's Shackle

<div class="tip-box">
<strong>💡 Pro Tip:</strong> Buy <strong>Margit's Shackle</strong> from Patches in Murkwater Cave (Limgrave river, follow the canyon north from Agheel Lake). It costs 5,000 Runes and lets you stun Margit twice during phase 1. This makes the fight significantly easier.
</div>

### Summon Spirit Ashes

Grab the **Spirit Calling Bell** from Renna at the Church of Elleh (fast travel there at night), and pick up **Lone Wolf Ashes** or **Jellyfish Ashes**. Summon them at the start of the fight — they'll draw aggro while you get damage in.

### NPC Summon: Sorcerer Rogier

Right outside the fog gate, you'll find a gold summon sign for **Sorcerer Rogier**. He casts Glintstone Sorceries and can do decent damage while staying alive reasonably well. Use him.

## Margit's Attack Patterns (and How to Counter)

Margit has two phases. Phase 2 starts at ~60% HP and adds holy weapon attacks.

### Phase 1 Attacks

| Attack | Tell | Counter |
|--------|------|---------|
| **Overhead Slam** | Raises cane high, pauses ~1.5s | Roll sideways just before impact. Punish with 1-2 hits. |
| **Double Slash** | Quick horizontal swing, then follow-up | Roll into the first swing, roll again for the second. |
| **Tail Sweep** | Spins body, tail extends | Roll through the tail (toward Margit). |
| **Dagger Throw** | Reaches for belt, throws 2 daggers rapidly | Sprint sideways or roll through each. |
| **Leaping Slam** | Jumps high, slams cane down | Roll toward him as he descends, get behind for free damage. |
| **Charged Cane Spin** | Holds cane behind him, spins forward | Roll backward twice — this attack tracks and has long range. |

### Phase 2 Attacks (Below 60% HP)

| Attack | Tell | Counter |
|--------|------|---------|
| **Holy Sword Combo** | Summons golden sword + hammer, unleashes 3-hit combo | Roll through each swing. The third hit has a long wind-up — don't roll early. |
| **Holy Hammer Slam** | Raises golden hammer, slams ground | Roll sideways at the last moment. Watch for the follow-up sword swipe. |
| **Dagger Barrage** | Throws 4+ daggers rapidly | Sprint in a circle, or roll spam through the volley. |

### Key Principle: Don't Roll on Reaction

Margit is designed to punish panic rolling. His attacks have **intentional delays** — he'll raise his cane, hold it for 1-2 seconds, THEN swing. The trick is:

1. Watch the cane/hammer, not his body
2. Listen for the sound cue (a grunt or whoosh)
3. Roll just before impact, not when he starts moving

## Strategy: How to Beat Margit Consistently

### Phase 1: Be Patient, Use Shackle

1. Enter fog, immediately roll forward (he often opens with a leaping slam)
2. Summon Spirit Ashes and Rogier
3. **Use Margit's Shackle** — it pins him to the ground for ~4 seconds
4. Get in 3-4 hits (or a full charged heavy) while he's pinned
5. Let summons take aggro; attack his back
6. Conserve your healing for phase 2
7. You can use Shackle a second time in phase 1

### Phase 2: Play Defensive, Punish Safely

1. At 60% HP, he'll roar and summon holy weapons — <strong>back off immediately</strong>
2. His combos are longer now (3-5 hits). Wait for a clear end before attacking
3. **Safest punish window**: after his leaping hammer slam
4. **Second safest**: after the holy sword + hammer combo finishes
5. Only hit 1-2 times then reset — <strong>greed is the #1 cause of death</strong>
6. If Rogier/Spirits die, play extra cautious

### What NOT to Do

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>⚠️ Common Mistakes:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Don't stand at medium range — it's where his daggers and leaping attacks hit hardest. Stay close or far away.</li>
<li>Don't heal while locked on and standing still — Margit will instantly punish with daggers. Unlock and sprint away first.</li>
<li>Don't use the Shackle in phase 2 — it doesn't work anymore.</li>
</ul>
</div>

## Recommended Builds for This Fight

### Melee (Strength/Dexterity)

- Weap up a Straight Sword or Curved Sword to +3
- Use **Square Off** Ash of War — the heavy version does massive stance damage
- Shield with 100% physical block helps if you're learning patterns

### Magic (Astrologer)

- **Glintstone Pebble** from range while summons tank
- **Carian Slicer** if he closes distance
- Stay at maximum lock-on range at all times

### Faith/Incantations

- **Flame Sling** or **Black Flame** does solid damage
- Use a shield with 100% physical block in off-hand

## After the Fight

Defeating Margit gives you:
- 12,000 Runes
- **Talisman Pouch** — an extra talisman slot!

Proceed through the tunnel to enter Stormveil Castle proper. Good luck, Tarnished.
