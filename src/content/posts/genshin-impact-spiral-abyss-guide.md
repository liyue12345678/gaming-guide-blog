Spiral Abyss is Genshin's ultimate test — two teams, three chambers per floor, and a DPS check that gets harder every patch. But 36-star clears aren't just for whales. With the right strategy, F2P players can consistently clear.

## How Spiral Abyss Works

| Floor | Difficulty | Primogems | Resets |
|-------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| 1–8 | Fixed, easy | 2400 (one-time) | Never |
| 9–10 | Moderate | 300 per reset | Every 2 weeks |
| 11–12 | Hard | 300 per reset | Every 2 weeks |

**9 stars per floor = 3 stars per chamber. Total: 36 stars = 600 primos per reset.**

## The Fundamental Strategy: Check the Blessing First

Every reset has a **Blessing of the Abyssal Moon** — a buff that cycles. Read it before building teams.

| Blessing Type | Best Teams to Use |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Normal Attack bonus | Yoimiya, Ayato, Wanderer, Wriothesley |
| Charged Attack bonus | Neuvillette, Ganyu, Tighnari, Hu Tao |
| Skill damage bonus | Fischl, Yae Miko, Navia, Furina |
| Burst damage bonus | Raiden, Xiangling, Xingqiu, Yelan |
| Elemental Reaction bonus | Dendro teams, Vape, Melt |
| HP fluctuation | Neuvillette, Furina, Wriothesley, Xiao |

<strong>💡 Key Insight:</strong> Even if the blessing doesn't match your main DPS, a 3-star clear is still possible with proper investment. The blessing helps — but it's never mandatory unless your artifacts are borderline.

## Team Building — The Two-Sides Problem

You need two completely independent teams with zero character overlap. Here's how to build them.

### Side 1 and Side 2 Checklist

For each team, make sure you have:

- ✅ **Main DPS** — your primary damage source
- ✅ **Sub-DPS / Reaction enabler** — off-field damage
- ✅ **Support / Buffer** — VV shred, Bennett buff, Kazuha grouping
- ✅ **Sustain** — Healer or strong shielder

### Example F2P-Friendly Split

| Role | Team 1 (Hyperbloom) | Team 2 (National) |
|------|---------------------|-------------------|
| DPS | Alhaitham / Nahida | Raiden (EM build) |
| Enabler | Xingqiu | Xiangling |
| Support | Kuki Shinobu (full EM) | Bennett |
| Flex | Dendro MC / Yaoyao | Xingqiu / Yelan / Kazuha |

### Premium Team Templates

**Team 1: Neuvillette Hypercarry**
```
Neuvillette + Furina + Kazuha + Baizhu/Zhongli
```
Best single-target damage in the game. Works on any side. Weak to Hydro-immune enemies.

**Team 2: Vape/Melt**
```
Hu Tao / Arlecchino + Yelan + Xingqiu + Zhongli
or
Wriothesley / Ganyu + Xiangling + Bennett + Zhongli
```
Strong single-target, great for boss chambers.

**F2P Powerhouse: Hyperbloom**
```
Dendro DPS + Xingqiu + Kuki Shinobu (1000+ EM) + flex
```
Lowest investment-to-damage ratio in the game. Level Kuki to 90 and stack EM — that's it.

## Chamber-Specific Strategies

### Dealing with Boss Chambers (Usually 12-2 and 12-3)

Bosses have high HP but predictable patterns. Key tips:
- **Single-target teams** over AoE teams
- Learn the boss's **stun phase** — most bosses take extra damage after a specific mechanic
- **Dodge** — 3-star clears require staying alive, not just dealing damage
- Reset if the boss uses an invulnerability phase that wastes time

### Dealing with Multi-Wave Chambers (Usually 12-1)

Waves of enemies that spawn sequentially. Key tips:
- **AoE damage** and **grouping** (Kazuha, Venti, Sucrose) are essential
- Position yourself so new enemies spawn within your damage field
- Don't use your burst on the last enemy of a wave — save it for the next spawn

### Common Floor 12 Boss Matchups

| Boss | Counters | Avoid |
|------|----------|-------|
| Wenut / Serpent | Fast elemental application, bow characters | Slow setup teams |
| Maguu Kenki | Shields, ranged damage | Melee without shields |
| Triple Kenki | AoE damage, grouping, strong healing | Squishy teams |
| Aeonblight Drake | Bow character mandatory | No archer = can't hit weak points |
| Baptist (elemental shields) | Multiple elements on team | Mono-element teams |

## Energy Management — The Hidden Timer

Missed bursts = lost DPS = lost stars. Before entering a chamber:

1. **Pre-funnel energy** — if the next chamber needs Raiden's burst ready, funnel particles to her before finishing the current chamber
2. **Hold bursts** — if enemies are at 5% HP, auto-attack them down; don't waste bursts that won't recharge before the next chamber
3. **Favonius weapons** — the single best investment for supports. A Fav Sword on Kazuha or Xingqiu lowers ER requirements for the entire team

## The "Three Attempts" Rule

You don't need to 3-star every chamber in one run. The game counts your best star count per chamber independently:

1. **First run**: Build a team optimized for Chamber 1. Get 3 stars. Play through Chambers 2 and 3 casually.
2. **Second run**: Rebuild for Chamber 2. Get 3 stars. (You already have Chamber 1's stars locked in.)
3. **Third run**: Rebuild for Chamber 3. Get 3 stars.

This means you can use different teams for each chamber. Exploit this.

## When to Reset

| Situation | Reset? |
|-----------|--------|
| A character dies in the first 30 seconds | Yes — restart immediately |
| You miss a crit on your nuke setup | Yes — if it costs you the chamber time |
| You're 10 seconds over the 3-star timer | Yes — but adjust strategy, don't just repeat |
| You cleared but only got 2 stars | Maybe — try once more with optimized rotation |
| You got 3 stars but someone died | No — stars are locked in, move on |
