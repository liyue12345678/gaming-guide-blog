Honour Mode is no joke. One full party wipe and your run ends — or continues in "dishonour" without the golden dice reward. Bosses have legendary actions, Haste gives only one extra attack, and damage riders work differently.

Here's how to survive.

## The Golden Rule: Always Have an Escape Plan

Before every fight, ask yourself: "If this goes badly, how do I get out?"

- Always keep one character at range with **Misty Step** or **Dimension Door** prepared
- Have at least one **Invisibility Potion** on every character
- Keep a **Scroll of Revivify** outside your dead character's inventory
- If 3 party members go down, the 4th runs. Don't try to be a hero.

## Best Honour Mode Party Composition

| Role | Best Pick | Alternative |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **Face / Control** | Swords Bard 10 / Paladin 2 | Lore Bard |
| **Striker** | Open Hand Monk 9 / Thief 3 | Battle Master Fighter 12 |
| **Blaster** | Storm Sorcerer 10 / Tempest Cleric 2 | Draconic Sorcerer 12 |
| **Flex / Support** | Life Cleric 12 | Light Cleric 12 |

### Why This Works

- **Swords Bard** handles dialogue, locks, and can nova with Slashing Flourish + Smite
- **Open Hand Monk** stuns priority targets and deals the highest single-target DPR in the game
- **Storm Sorcerer** twin-casts Haste on the Bard and Monk, then blasts with Lightning
- **Life Cleric** keeps everyone alive with Preserve Life channel divinity — a full party heal on short rest

## The Most Dangerous Fights (and How to Survive)

### 1. Inquisitor W'wargaz (Act 1, Githyanki Crèche)

This fight ends more Honour Mode runs than any other in Act 1.

**Why it's deadly**: Four summoned swords with psychic damage, and the Inquisitor's multi-attack can one-round a character.

**How to win**:
- Drink **Elixirs of Psychic Resistance** on all 4 characters before entering
- Kill the summoned swords first — they each have low HP
- Burst the Inquisitor before he takes a second turn
- Come at level 6+, not 5

### 2. Apostle of Myrkul (Act 2)

**Why it's deadly**: Large AoE scythe, Bone Chill aura (no healing), and legendary action that pulls characters into melee.

**How to win**:
- Free Dame Aylin on turn 1 with a fast character (Thief Rogue bonus action)
- Put **Wet** condition on the Apostle (throw water or Create Water) + Lightning damage = double damage
- Stay on the outer platforms to avoid the scythe AoE
- Spirit Guardians (Radiant) from the Cleric melts the adds

### 3. Cazador (Act 3)

**Why it's deadly**: Legendary action mist form and massive necrotic damage.

**How to win**:
- Cast **Daylight** before the fight — it nullifies his mist form
- Leave Astarion on the stairs (don't trigger the cutscene with him in party)
- Focus Cazador immediately, ignore the spawns

### 4. Raphael (Act 3, House of Hope)

**Why it's deadly**: 666 HP, ascendant fiends with charm, massive fire AoE.

**How to win**:
- Use the **Fountains of Restoration** in the boudoir to long-rest mid-dungeon
- Destroy the 4 Soul Pillars with Force damage on turn 1 (Eldritch Blast, Magic Missile)
- Hope (the NPC) has a unique Divine Intervention — save it for emergency
- Globe of Invulnerability scroll in the center of the room = easy mode

### 5. The Netherbrain (Final Boss)

**Why it's deadly**: Platforms get deleted, mind flayers spam Magic Missile, and the brain's psychic blast can wipe the party.

**How to win**:
- Everyone drinks a **Potion of Speed** before climbing the brain stem
- Use the **Orphic Ring** mechanic — one character channels, others protect them
- Globe of Invulnerability on the channeler
- Blow all your saved scrolls and consumables — this is the last fight

## Honour Mode-Specific Rules to Remember

1. **Haste gives only 1 extra attack** (not a full action). Potions of Speed still good, but less broken.
2. **Damage rider stacking is fixed** — no more DRS abuse with Phalar Aluve + Magic Missile.
3. **Legendary actions** on all major bosses. Read the examine window before engaging.
4. **Single save file** — no reloading. Embrace the consequences.
5. **Chained attacks work differently** — multi-hit attacks use the same attack roll, not independent rolls.

## Should You Skip Any Content?

**Yes.** In Honour Mode, the reward for optional bosses isn't always worth the risk:

| Boss | Fight It? | Reason |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Phase Spider Matriarch | Skip | No unique loot worth the risk of fall damage death |
| Bulette | Skip | Annoying, no unique drops |
| Ansur (Act 3) | **Skip** | Giant AoE one-shots squishies. Helm of Balduran is good but not run-ending without it. |
| Shambling Mound (Act 2) | Skip | Risky adds, mediocre reward |

<strong>⚠️ Honour Mode Golden Rule:</strong> If you're not 100% confident in a fight, skip it. You can always come back later. There's no prize for killing every optional boss — only for reaching the credits with the golden dice.
