Omen is the most versatile controller in Valorant. His reusable smokes, on-demand flash, and mobility make him a solo-carry agent. This guide covers how to play him at every rank.

## Why Omen Over Other Controllers?

| Feature | Omen | Brimstone | Astra | Viper |
|---------|------|-----------|-------|-------|
| Smokes refill | Every 30s | None (3 total) | Stars regen | Fuel-based |
| Flash | Yes (Paranoia) | No | No | No |
| Mobility | Yes (Teleport) | No | No | No |
| Global presence | Limited | Yes (iPad) | Yes | No |

Omen's smokes regenerate — you always have one ready. This alone makes him the best solo-queue controller.

## Ability Breakdown

### Dark Cover (E) — Signature Smoke
- **Free, regenerates every 30 seconds**
- Max range displayed on minimap
- Can be placed through walls
- Enters a shadow world view when placing — practice the timing in custom games

**Key tip**: Put one smoke slightly inside the choke, not at the entrance. This forces enemies to fully commit through smoke before seeing the site.

### Paranoia (Q) — Flash/Blind
- Travels through walls
- Hits everyone in its path — teammates included
- Reduces vision AND deafens (muffled sound)

**Key tip**: Use it through walls to blind common angles. On defense, throw it through the wall as attackers push a choke — they can't react.

### Shrouded Step (C) — Teleport
- Short-range teleport
- Makes a loud sound at the destination
- Can reach elevated positions

**One-Way Smoke + TP combo**: Smoke a doorway, teleport on top of the smoke, drop down behind them. Works once per game — then they'll check for it.

### From the Shadows (X) — Ultimate
- Teleports anywhere on the map
- Can be cancelled if spotted
- The shadow form makes a distinct sound

Use it for:
- **Flank rotations** — ult to the opposite site during a fake
- **Info gathering** — cancel if spotted, you know where enemies are
- **Spike recovery** — ult onto a dropped spike after your team dies

## Attack Side: Default Smoke Setups

### Ascent A Site
- Smoke **Tree** (blocks defender rotation)
- Smoke **Heaven** (blocks elevated angle)
- Paranoia through **A Main door** → push out
- TP onto **Generator** for unexpected angle

### Haven C Site
- Smoke **Garage** (blocks rotation from B)
- Smoke **CT spawn entrance**
- Paranoia through the right wall of C Long
- Default plant safe behind the metal box

## Defense Side: Delay, Don't Fight

Omen's job on defense is to delay the push, not to peek. Your smokes make attackers hesitate:

1. **Smoke the choke** immediately at round start
2. **Paranoia** through the smoke when footsteps are close
3. **Fall back** to site and smoke again
4. If they push through 2 smokes and paranoia, your team should be fully rotated

## One-Way Smokes Worth Learning

| Map | Position | Lineup |
|-----|----------|--------|
| Ascent | A Tree to A Main | Place smoke on top edge of entrance arch |
| Bind | B Hookah | Smoke on the door ledge inside Hookah |
| Split | B Heaven | Smoke on the garage door frame |
| Pearl | B Link | Smoke on the arch lip |

Each forces attackers to crouch through smoke, exposing their feet before they can see you.

## Tips for Climbing

<ol>
<li><strong>Communicate your smokes</strong> — Say "smoking Heaven" before you place it so your duelist can position</li>
<li><strong>Don't lurk every round</strong> — Your smokes are more valuable than one pick</li>
<li><strong>Learn 2 one-way smokes per map</strong> — More than that and you'll forget them under pressure</li>
<li><strong>Cancel your ult if someone is near</strong> — 7 ult points isn't worth dying for</li>
<li><strong>Paranoia after your duelist's flash</strong> — Double-blind is nearly impossible to dodge</li>
</ol>
