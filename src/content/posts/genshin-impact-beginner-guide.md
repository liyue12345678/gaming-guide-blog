Genshin Impact is massive, and its systems can be overwhelming for new players. These 12 tips will save you months of wasted resources.

## 1. Do NOT Spend Primogems on the Standard Banner

This is the #1 newbie mistake. The standard banner (Wanderlust Invocation) has no rate-up for specific 5-star characters. Always save your Primogems for the **limited-time character banners**. Use only the free Acquaint Fates on the standard banner.

## 2. Understand the Pity System

- **Soft pity**: 74 wishes — rate starts climbing sharply
- **Hard pity**: 90 wishes — guaranteed 5-star
- **50/50**: Your first 5-star has a 50% chance of being the featured character
- **Guaranteed**: If you lose the 50/50, your next 5-star is 100% the featured character
- Pity carries over between limited banners of the same type

## 3. Don't Farm Artifacts Before AR45

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>⚠️ Biggest Mistake:</strong> Using resin on artifact domains before AR45. At AR45, you unlock the highest domain level, which guarantees a 5-star artifact every run. Farming before AR45 wastes resin on 4-star artifacts you'll replace immediately.
</div>

Before AR45, spend resin on:
- **Weekly bosses** (Trounce Domains) — for talent materials and billets
- **Talent domains** — guaranteed character power increase
- **Weapon ascension materials** — guaranteed weapon power increase
- **Mora and XP leylines** — you will ALWAYS need these

## 4. Build One Team at a Time

Don't spread resources across 10 characters. Focus on 4 characters first:
- 1 main DPS (your best damage dealer)
- 1 sub-DPS (off-field damage or reactions)
- 1 support/healer
- 1 flex (buffer, shielder, crowd control)

Get your main DPS to level 80/90 with level 8 talents before investing heavily in anyone else.

## 5. Daily Priority Checklist

1. Spend resin (160 resin = 8 domain runs or 4 boss runs)
2. Complete 4 daily commissions (60 Primogems per day)
3. Send characters on expeditions (free ore and food)
4. Claim commission rewards from Katheryne
5. Battle Pass daily missions if you bought it

## 6. Elemental Reaction Basics

| Reaction | Elements | Effect |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Vaporize | Pyro + Hydro | 1.5x or 2x damage multiplier |
| Melt | Pyro + Cryo | 1.5x or 2x damage multiplier |
| Aggravate | Electro + Dendro | Bonus flat damage |
| Hyperbloom | Dendro + Hydro + Electro | Homing missiles |
| Freeze | Hydro + Cryo | Freezes enemies solid |

**Dendro teams** (Hyperbloom especially) are the most F2P-friendly — they deal high damage with low investment.

## 7. Don't Refine 4-Star Weapons Until You Have Duplicates

Many 4-star weapons are excellent and hard to replace. The **Favonius** and **Sacrificial** series weapons are top-tier support weapons. A second copy is often more valuable than R2 on your first copy.

## 8. Mark Your Map

Use pins! Mark:
- Oculi locations (so you don't miss any)
- Artifact investigation spots (free artifact XP)
- Mining outcrops (for weapon XP crystals)
- Fishing spots (for The Catch and other weapons)

## 9. The Spiral Abyss Isn't Going Anywhere

Don't stress about clearing Abyss early. Floors 1-8 are one-time rewards. Floors 9-12 reset every 2 weeks but require 2 strong teams. Most players don't 36-star Abyss until AR55+.

## 10. Save Fragile Resin for AR45+

Every Fragile Resin you get before AR45 should be saved. At AR45, you can burn them all on artifact domains and get 5-star pieces every run.

## 11. Buy the Battle Pass Weapon If You Need It

The $10 Battle Pass weapons are very strong:
- **Serpent Spine** — best 4-star claymore in the game
- **The Black Sword** — excellent for sword DPS
- **Deathmatch** — solid polearm DPS option

If you're F2P, craftable weapons like **Prototype Rancour** and **Whiteblind** are serviceable.

## 12. Events Give the Most Rewards

Limited-time events are your #1 source of Primogems outside dailies and exploration. Always do events — they often give enough Primogems for 5-10 wishes plus crowns, weapons, and materials.
