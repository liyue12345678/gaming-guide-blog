The Swords Bard is arguably the strongest build in Baldur's Gate 3. It excels at everything — dialogue, crowd control, burst damage, and survivability. This guide covers the optimal level split, gear, and combat rotation.

## Why Swords Bard?

- **Slashing Flourish** deals damage to two targets or hits one target twice — with Sharpshooter, that's insane
- **Bardic Inspiration** recharges on short rest from level 5 (Song of Rest = 3 short rests per day)
- **Expertise** in Persuasion and Sleight of Hand makes you the ultimate face
- **Full spellcaster** progression for upcasting and utility spells
- **Helmet of Arcane Acuity** breaks bounded accuracy — +7 spell DC in one turn

## Level Split

### Core Build: Swords Bard 10 / Paladin 2

| Level | Class | Key Features |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Bard | Spells, Bardic Inspiration (d6) |
| 2 | Bard | Song of Rest, Jack of All Trades |
| 3 | Bard (Swords) | Fighting Style: Two-Weapon or Dueling, Blade Flourish |
| 4 | Bard | Feat: Sharpshooter |
| 5 | Bard | Font of Inspiration, d8 Inspiration |
| 6 | Bard | Extra Attack |
| 7 | Paladin 1 | Heavy Armor, Divine Sense |
| 8 | Paladin 2 | Divine Smite, Fighting Style: Defense |
| 9-12 | Bard 7-10 | Magical Secrets, d10 Inspiration |

### Alternative: 6 Swords Bard / 2 Paladin / 4 Thief Rogue

Trades spellcasting for an extra bonus action. Better sustained damage, fewer smites.

## Race Choice

- **Halfling** — Lucky trait means you almost never crit-fail
- **Wood Elf / Half-Elf** — Extra movement speed
- **Duergar Dwarf** — Free invisibility at level 5

Use the **Hag's Hair (+1 DEX)** in Act 1 to reach 20 DEX by level 4.

## Key Gear Progression

### Act 1
- **Titanstring Bow** (Zhentarim merchant) — adds STR mod to damage
- **Gloves of Archery** (Goblin Camp vendor) — +2 ranged damage
- **Caustic Band** (Myconid Colony) — +2 acid damage per hit
- **Club of Hill Giant Strength** (Arcane Tower) — sets STR to 19 for Titanstring synergy

### Act 2
- **Helmet of Arcane Acuity** (Mason's Guild) — THE build-defining item
- **Risky Ring** (Araj Oblodra) — advantage on all attacks
- **Band of the Mystic Scoundrel** (Jungle area) — cast illusion/enchantment spells as bonus action

### Act 3
- **Bhaalist Armor** (Orin quest) — enemies in melee range are vulnerable to piercing (double damage!)
- **Dead Shot** (Fytz the Firecracker) — improved critical, better than Titanstring at this point

## Combat Rotation

1. **Turn 1**: Slashing Flourish (Ranged) × 4 → stack Arcane Acuity to +8 → Bonus Action: Hold Person / Hold Monster
2. **Turn 2+**: Auto-crit on held targets → Divine Smite on every hit

With Haste and Bloodlust Elixir, you get 8 attacks in one turn. Against held targets, each hit is an auto-crit smite. Nothing survives this.

## Magical Secrets (Bard 10)

- **Counterspell** — mandatory for Honour Mode
- **Hunger of Hadar or Spirit Guardians** — your call
