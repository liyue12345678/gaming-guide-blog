Artifact farming is the biggest resin sink in Genshin Impact — and the place where most players waste the most time. This guide will teach you the efficient farming strategy that top players actually use.

## The Golden Rule: Don't Farm Before AR45

If you take one thing from this guide: **never spend resin on artifact domains before Adventure Rank 45.**

At AR45, the highest domain level unlocks and guarantees a 5-star artifact every 20 resin. Before AR45, you're gambling on 4-star pieces you'll replace within weeks. Spend pre-AR45 resin on:
- Talent books (guaranteed power)
- Weapon ascension materials (guaranteed power)
- Mora and XP leylines (you need millions of mora at endgame)

## How to Evaluate an Artifact in 3 Seconds

When you see a 5-star artifact drop, check these three things in order:

| Priority | What to Check | Good | Bad |
|----------|--------------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Main stat** | Is it the right one? | Def% on a DPS piece = instant trash |
| 2 | **Substats (level 0)** | 2+ useful substats? | 0-1 useful stats = fodder |
| 3 | **Rolls at +4 and +8** | Did useful stats get upgraded? | 2+ rolls into flat DEF = stop rolling |

If an artifact fails at step 1 or 2: use it as leveling fodder. If it fails at step 3: stop leveling it and feed it to the next piece.

## The "2 Good Substats" Rule

Here's the filter that top theorycrafters use:

**At level 0, count the artifact's useful substats:**

| Starting Substats | Action |
|-------------------|--------|
| 3-4 useful | Level to +4, then decide |
| 2 useful | Level to +4, only continue if both rolls hit good stats |
| 0-1 useful | Fodder immediately |

"Useful substats" depends on the character:

| Character Type | Good Substats | Priority |
|---------------|--------------|----------|
| ATK-scaling DPS | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK% | CRIT > ATK% |
| HP-scaling DPS | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, HP% | CRIT > HP% |
| DEF-scaling DPS | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, DEF% | CRIT > DEF% |
| EM-focused (Dendro) | EM, ER | EM > ER |
| Pure Support | ER, HP%/DEF% (for shields) | ER until requirement met |

## The Flower and Feather Are Your Best Pieces

Flowers always have HP main stat, feathers always have ATK main stat. Since the main stat is fixed, you only need to roll good substats. This makes them **3-4x more likely to be good** than goblets.

<div class="tip-box">
<strong>💡 Pro Tip:</strong> Your best artifacts should almost always be your flower and feather. If they're not, you're getting unlucky or your standards are too high for sands/goblet/circlet.
</div>

## Goblets Are the Bottleneck — Use Off-Pieces

The goblet slot has 11 possible main stats (Elemental DMG bonuses + physical + EM + ATK/HP/DEF%). Getting the right Elemental DMG% goblet from the right set is a statistical nightmare.

**Solution**: Your goblet is almost always your **off-piece**. Farm for good goblets across all domains and use them as the flexible 5th slot in any 4-piece set. A Hydro DMG goblet with double crit from the Maiden Beloved set is better than a Hydro DMG goblet with DEF substats that's on-set.

## The Strongbox: Your Secret Weapon

At the crafting table, you can convert three 5-star artifacts into one from a classic set. **Always strongbox bad artifacts** — never use 5-star artifacts as fodder XP. You can farm fodder XP from the overworld artifact investigation spots (free, respawn daily).

Best sets to strongbox:
1. **Emblem of Severed Fate** — best value, used by 20+ characters
2. **Noblesse Oblige** — universal support set
3. **Viridescent Venerer** — mandatory for Anemo supports
4. **Crimson Witch of Flames** — for Pyro DPS characters

## When to Stop Farming for a Character

Don't chase perfect artifacts. Here's when to move on:

| Investment Level | CRIT Ratio (no weapon) | Stop Here For |
|------------------|------------------------|---------------|
| Minimal | 50/100 | Support characters, off-field DPS |
| Good | 60/120 | Most main DPS, Abyss ready |
| Great | 70/140 | Your favorite characters |
| Perfect | 80/180+ | Only if you have nothing else to farm |

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>⚠️ Diminishing Returns:</strong> Going from 60/120 to 70/140 takes about 3x more resin than going from 50/100 to 60/120. At some point, leveling talents to 9/9/9 gives more damage than another week of artifact farming.
</div>

## Daily Artifact Investigation Route (Free XP)

There are ~130 artifact investigation spots in the overworld that respawn every 24 hours. Each gives 1-2 star artifacts you can use as fodder XP. A 15-minute daily route nets enough XP to level one artifact to +20 every few days — without spending any resin.

Best zones for investigation route:
1. **Seirai Island shipwreck** — highest density of spots
2. **Tatarasuna** — lots of crates and boxes
3. **Dragonspine camps** — good if you can handle the cold

## Summary: The Efficient Farmer's Checklist

1. ✅ Don't touch artifact domains until AR45
2. ✅ Farm for 2+ useful substats at level 0 — fodder anything with 0-1
3. ✅ Stop rolling at +8 if you've already missed 2+ rolls
4. ✅ Your goblet is always the off-piece
5. ✅ Strongbox all bad 5-stars, never use them as fodder XP
6. ✅ Stop at 60/120 crit for non-favorite characters and move on
7. ✅ Do the daily investigation route for free artifact XP
