Yellow Loong is widely considered the hardest secret boss in Black Myth: Wukong. This hidden dragon in Chapter 4 tests everything you've learned — and then some. The community rates him 9.5/10 on the difficulty scale for good reason.

Here's exactly how to beat him.

## Where to Find Yellow Loong

Yellow Loong is a secret boss in Chapter 4's **Webbed Hollow**. To reach him:

1. Progress through the Webbed Hollow until you reach the **Pool of Shattered Jade** shrine
2. Look for a hidden path behind a waterfall near the shrine
3. Follow the cave path to the cliffside arena
4. Interact with the hanging painting to trigger the fight

<div class="warning-box">
<strong>⚠️ Note:</strong> This is an optional secret boss. If you're struggling, you can return after completing Chapter 5 or 6 with better gear. Many players recommend coming back with the Chapter 6 Wukong armor set.
</div>

## Recommended Build

### Weapons & Armor
- **Weapon**: Jingubang (if available) or any max-upgraded pillar with high crit
- **Armor**: Pilgrim Set for the movement speed and crit bonus, or Gold Suozi Set for defense
- **Spirit**: Wandering Wight (upgraded, for the headbutt stun)

### Spells & Abilities
| Slot | Choice | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| Spell 1 | **Immobilize** | Creates the only reliable damage window |
| Spell 2 | **Rock Solid** | Parries his aerial dive attacks perfectly |
| Spell 3 | **Cloud Step** | Escape his grab and lightning AoE |
| Spell 4 | **Ring of Fire** | Heal in safety, boost charged attacks |
| Transformation | **Red Tides** (Guangzhi) | Fire damage chips through his defense |
| Vessel | **Wind Tamer** | Interrupts his aerial lightning summoning |

### Curios & Medicine
- **Gold Sun Crow** + **Jade Moon Rabbit** combo
- Bring **Shock-Quelling Powder** — his lightning attacks build Shock fast
- **Tiger Subduing Pellets** for the damage buff

## Yellow Loong's Attack Patterns

Yellow Loong has three phases. He gets faster and adds more lightning attacks as he loses HP.

### Phase 1 (100% – 70% HP): Physical Melee

| Attack | Tell | How to Counter |
|--------|------|---------------|
| **Glaive Combo (3 hits)** | Low stance, then rapid swings | Rock Solid the first hit; dodge sideways for the follow-ups |
| **Dashing Thrust** | Glaive pulled back, brief charge | Dodge **late** — he has a tracking delay |
| **Overhead Slam** | Glaive raised high, then slam | Side-strafe then punish with 2-3 light hits |
| **Teleporting Grab** | Disappears in black smoke, audio cue (scream) | **Dodge the instant you hear the scream**, not when you see him |
| **Tail Whip** | Turns body, tail extends | Roll through the tail toward him for free back damage |

**Strategy**: Rock Solid is your best friend in Phase 1. Parry his combos, counter with a heavy attack, then back off. Don't get greedy — he punishes extended combos with a grab.

### Phase 2 (70% – 30% HP): Lightning Empowered

Yellow Loong starts infusing his glaive with lightning. All attacks now deal **Shock buildup**. If Shock procs, you take bonus damage and move slower.

| New Attack | Tell | How to Counter |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Lightning Glaive Throw** | Spins glaive above head, then hurls it | Sprint perpendicular to the throw line |
| **Ground Lightning Wave** | Slams glaive into ground, lightning spreads in a cone | Jump over the wave, or Cloud Step through it |
| **Lightning Dive** | Leaps into the air, targets you with lightning trail | Rock Solid mid-air — one of the most satisfying parries in the game |
| **Multi-Dash Combo** | Dashes 4-5 times in quick succession | Count the dashes — dodge rhythmically, punish after the 5th |

**Strategy**: This is the longest phase. Use **Ring of Fire** to create a safe zone. Charged heavy attacks from inside the ring deal extra damage. Pop Shock-Quelling Powder when the Shock bar fills halfway. Use Wind Tamer to knock him out of aerial attacks.

### Phase 3 (Below 30% HP): Desperation Mode

Yellow Loong becomes hyper-aggressive. His attack windows shrink significantly.

**Changes**:
- Combos extend from 3 hits to 5-6 hits
- Grab becomes faster with less telegraph
- Lightning AoE radius doubles
- He chains abilities — grab → lightning wave → dive

<div class="tip-box">
<strong>💡 Phase 3 Tip:</strong> This is where most players die. Save your Transformation, Pluck of Many (if unlocked), and all medicine buffs for this phase. The goal is to burst him down before he overwhelms you with chain attacks.
</div>

**Strategy**:
1. Pop all damage buffs (Tiger Subduing + damage curios)
2. Cast Immobilize → full charged heavy combo
3. Transform into Red Tides → spam fire attacks
4. Wind Tamer to cancel his first aerial attack
5. Cloud Step to avoid the inevitable grab attempt
6. If he's still alive, play ultra-defensive and punish only after his dive

## The "Cheese" Strategy

If you want to beat Yellow Loong without mastering every parry:

1. Equip **Thrust Stance** (not Smash)
2. Equip **Ring of Fire** and upgrade it fully
3. At the start of the fight, cast Ring of Fire immediately
4. Stay inside the ring and spam **charged Thrust Stance heavy attacks**
5. The ring heals you and boosts charged attacks — you can trade hits and win
6. Use Cloud Step whenever the ring expires to buy time for the cooldown
7. Recast Ring of Fire and repeat

This strategy works because Thrust Stance's charged attack out-ranges most of Yellow Loong's melee attacks. Combined with Ring of Fire's healing, you effectively out-sustain him.
